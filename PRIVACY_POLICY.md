# Privacy Policy for CopifyX

**Last Updated: October 2023**

CopifyX ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application.

## 1. No Data Collection
CopifyX is designed with a "Privacy by Design" approach. 
- **We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data.**
- **We do not have access to the Internet.** The App does not request the `INTERNET` permission, meaning it is technically impossible for the App to send any information from your device to any external servers.
- All text processing and selection happen locally on your device.

## 2. Accessibility Service Usage
CopifyX requires the use of the **Accessibility Service API** to function. This is a sensitive permission that you must explicitly grant.

### Why do we need it?
The Accessibility Service is used solely to:
- Read the content of the current screen to identify text elements.
- Allow you to select and copy text from apps that otherwise prevent standard text selection.

### How is it used?
- The service only activates its "screen reading" capability when you manually trigger the "Grab" function via the floating button or Quick Settings tile.
- **Data processed via the Accessibility Service is never saved, uploaded, or shared.** It is used momentarily to display a text selection overlay and is discarded immediately after you close the overlay or copy text to your clipboard.

## 3. Third-Party Services
The App does not use any third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking tools.

## 4. Permissions
In addition to the Accessibility Service, the App may use the following:
- **Foreground Service**: To keep the floating "Grab" button active and responsive.
- **System Alert Window (Overlay)**: To display the text selection interface over other apps.

## 5. Security
Because the App operates entirely offline and does not store any data, your information remains within the secure environment of your own device.

## 6. Children's Privacy
Since we do not collect any data, our App does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Since the App does not have internet access, we recommend checking the store listing or the App's "About" section for any updates.
